The Science

Electrolysis, simplified.

1

Add Water

Pour clean tap or distilled water into the inlet port on top. That's the only ingredient you need.

2

Electrolysis

Our PEM cell splits water molecules using a low-voltage current, separating hydrogen from oxygen.

3

Pure Hydrogen

99.9% pure hydrogen gas flows through the output port, ready to use immediately.
